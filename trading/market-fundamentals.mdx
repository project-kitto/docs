  </p>

  <div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <div className="bg-slate-800 p-4 rounded-lg border border-slate-700">

          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Strategy:</strong> Begin accumulating positions in quality companies</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div className="bg-slate-800 p-4 rounded-lg border border-slate-700">

          <Icon icon="trending-up" size={16} />
          2. Markup Phase
        </h4>
        <div className="space-y-2 text-sm">
          <p className="text-slate-300"><strong>Characteristics:</strong> Rising prices, improving sentiment, expanding valuations</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Public Entry:</strong> Retail investors start participating</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Duration:</strong> 1-3 years in major cycles</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Strategy:</strong> Hold positions, avoid over-trading</p>

          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Strategy:</strong> Begin taking profits, increase cash positions</p>

      </div>

      <div className="bg-slate-800 p-4 rounded-lg border border-slate-700">

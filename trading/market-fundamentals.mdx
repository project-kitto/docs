## Market Cycles: Understanding the Rhythm of Markets

<div className="bg-slate-900 p-6 rounded-xl border border-slate-800 mb-8">
  <h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-slate-100 mb-4">The Four Phases of Market Cycles</h3>
  <p className="text-slate-300 mb-6">
    Markets don't move in straight lines—they follow predictable cycles driven by economic conditions, investor psychology, and monetary policy. Understanding these cycles helps you make better investment timing decisions and manage risk appropriately.
  </p>

  <div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <div className="bg-slate-800 p-4 rounded-lg border border-slate-700">
        <h4 className="text-green-400 font-semibold mb-2 flex items-center gap-2">
          <Icon icon="seedling" size={16} />
          1. Accumulation Phase
        </h4>
        <div className="space-y-2 text-sm">
          <p className="text-slate-300"><strong>Characteristics:</strong> Market bottoming, high pessimism, low valuations</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Smart Money:</strong> Institutional investors start buying quality assets</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Duration:</strong> 3-12 months typically</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Strategy:</strong> Begin accumulating positions in quality companies</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div className="bg-slate-800 p-4 rounded-lg border border-slate-700">
        <h4 className="text-blue-400 font-semibold mb-2 flex items-center gap-2">
          <Icon icon="trending-up" size={16} />
          2. Markup Phase
        </h4>
        <div className="space-y-2 text-sm">
          <p className="text-slate-300"><strong>Characteristics:</strong> Rising prices, improving sentiment, expanding valuations</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Public Entry:</strong> Retail investors start participating</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Duration:</strong> 1-3 years in major cycles</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Strategy:</strong> Hold positions, avoid over-trading</p>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div className="space-y-4">
      <div className="bg-slate-800 p-4 rounded-lg border border-slate-700">
        <h4 className="text-amber-400 font-semibold mb-2 flex items-center gap-2">
          <Icon icon="pause" size={16} />
          3. Distribution Phase
        </h4>
        <div className="space-y-2 text-sm">
          <p className="text-slate-300"><strong>Characteristics:</strong> Market topping, euphoria, stretched valuations</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Smart Money:</strong> Institutions start selling to retail investors</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Duration:</strong> 3-18 months typically</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Strategy:</strong> Begin taking profits, increase cash positions</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div className="bg-slate-800 p-4 rounded-lg border border-slate-700">
        <h4 className="text-red-400 font-semibold mb-2 flex items-center gap-2">
          <Icon icon="trending-down" size={16} />
          4. Decline Phase
        </h4>
        <div className="space-y-2 text-sm">
          <p className="text-slate-300"><strong>Characteristics:</strong> Falling prices, panic selling, fear dominates</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Public Exit:</strong> Retail investors sell at the worst times</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Duration:</strong> 6 months to 2 years</p>
          <p className="text-slate-400"><strong>Strategy:</strong> Preserve capital, prepare for next cycle</p>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

## Investment Psychology: Mastering Your Emotions

<div className="bg-slate-900 p-6 rounded-xl border border-slate-800 mb-8">
  <h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-slate-100 mb-4">The Psychology of Market Movements</h3>
  <p className="text-slate-300 mb-6">
    Markets are driven by human emotions—fear and greed being the primary forces. Understanding market psychology helps you avoid common behavioral traps that destroy wealth and instead position yourself to benefit from others' emotional mistakes.
  </p>

  <div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">

          <h5 className="text-slate-200 font-medium">Herd Mentality</h5>

          <h5 className="text-slate-200 font-medium">Emotional Discipline</h5>

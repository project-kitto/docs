## What is a Broker?

<div className="bg-slate-900/50 p-6 rounded-xl border border-slate-700 my-8">
  <div className="flex items-center gap-4 mb-4">
    <div className="w-12 h-12 bg-slate-800 rounded-xl flex items-center justify-center border border-slate-600">
      <Icon icon="building-columns" size={24} className="text-slate-300" />
    </div>
    <div>
      <h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-slate-200">Your Gateway to Markets</h3>
      <p className="text-slate-400 text-sm">Licensed intermediary that executes your trades</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <p className="text-slate-300">
    A broker is a licensed financial intermediary that facilitates the buying and selling of securities on behalf of investors. They provide access to stock exchanges and other financial markets that individual investors can't access directly.
  </p>

  <div className="bg-blue-900/20 border border-blue-800 p-4 rounded-lg mt-6">
    <h4 className="text-blue-300 font-semibold mb-2">Key Function</h4>
    <p className="text-blue-200 text-sm">
      Brokers act as the middleman between you and the stock market. When you place an order to buy Apple stock, your broker routes that order to the appropriate exchange and handles the settlement process.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>

## Key Trading Terms

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="💰 Bid & Ask" icon="handshake" color="#7c3aed">
    **Bid**: Highest price buyers are willing to pay

    **Ask**: Lowest price sellers are willing to accept

    **Spread**: Difference between bid and ask prices
  </Card>

  <Card title="💧 Volume & Liquidity" icon="droplet" color="#06b6d4">
    **Volume**: Number of shares traded in a period

    **Liquidity**: How easily you can buy/sell without affecting price

    Higher volume = Better liquidity
  </Card>

  <Card title="Market Cap" icon="building">
    **Market Capitalization** = Share Price × Outstanding Shares

    - **Large Cap**: > $10B
    - **Mid Cap**: $2B - $10B
    - **Small Cap**: < $2B
  </Card>

  <Card title="Volatility" icon="chart-line-up">
    Measure of price fluctuation over time

    - **High Volatility**: Large price swings
    - **Low Volatility**: Stable price movements

    Higher risk = Higher potential returns
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Valuation Metrics

<div className="bg-gradient-to-br from-violet-50 to-purple-50 dark:from-violet-900/20 dark:to-purple-900/20 p-6 rounded-xl border border-violet-200 dark:border-violet-800 my-8">
  <div className="text-center mb-6">
    <h4 className="text-lg font-bold text-slate-800 dark:text-slate-200 mb-2">📊 Key Financial Ratios</h4>
    <p className="text-slate-600 dark:text-slate-400 text-sm">Essential metrics for stock evaluation</p>
  </div>

  <div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">
    <div className="bg-white/70 dark:bg-slate-800/70 p-5 rounded-xl border border-violet-200 dark:border-violet-700">
      <div className="flex items-center gap-3 mb-3">
        <div className="w-8 h-8 bg-violet-500 rounded-lg flex items-center justify-center">
          <span className="text-white text-sm">P/E</span>
        </div>
        <h5 className="font-bold text-slate-800 dark:text-slate-200">Price-to-Earnings</h5>
      </div>

      <div className="bg-violet-50 dark:bg-violet-900/30 p-3 rounded-lg mb-3">
        <div className="text-xs text-violet-700 dark:text-violet-300 mb-1">Formula:</div>
        <div className="font-mono text-sm text-violet-800 dark:text-violet-200">Stock Price ÷ EPS</div>
      </div>

      <div className="text-sm text-slate-700 dark:text-slate-300">
        <div className="mb-2">Shows how much investors pay for each dollar of earnings</div>
        <div className="space-y-1 text-xs">
          <div className="text-emerald-600 dark:text-emerald-400">• Low P/E: Potentially undervalued</div>
          <div className="text-orange-600 dark:text-orange-400">• High P/E: Growth expectations</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div className="bg-white/70 dark:bg-slate-800/70 p-5 rounded-xl border border-violet-200 dark:border-violet-700">
      <div className="flex items-center gap-3 mb-3">
        <div className="w-8 h-8 bg-indigo-500 rounded-lg flex items-center justify-center">
          <span className="text-white text-sm">P/B</span>
        </div>
        <h5 className="font-bold text-slate-800 dark:text-slate-200">Price-to-Book</h5>
      </div>

      <div className="bg-indigo-50 dark:bg-indigo-900/30 p-3 rounded-lg mb-3">
        <div className="text-xs text-indigo-700 dark:text-indigo-300 mb-1">Formula:</div>
        <div className="font-mono text-sm text-indigo-800 dark:text-indigo-200">Stock Price ÷ Book Value</div>
      </div>

      <div className="text-sm text-slate-700 dark:text-slate-300">
        <div className="mb-2">Compares market value to book value</div>
        <div className="space-y-1 text-xs">
          <div className="text-emerald-600 dark:text-emerald-400">• P/B < 1: Below book value</div>
          <div className="text-blue-600 dark:text-blue-400">• P/B > 1: Market premium</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div className="mt-6 bg-slate-100 dark:bg-slate-800 p-4 rounded-lg">
    <div className="text-sm font-medium text-slate-700 dark:text-slate-300 mb-2">Example Calculation:</div>
    <div className="font-mono text-xs text-slate-600 dark:text-slate-400 space-y-1">
      <div>Stock Price: $100</div>
      <div>Earnings Per Share: $5</div>
      <div className="text-violet-600 dark:text-violet-400">P/E Ratio: $100 ÷ $5 = 20x</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

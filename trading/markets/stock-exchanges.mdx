## What is a Stock Exchange?

A stock exchange is a regulated marketplace where securities like stocks, bonds, and other financial instruments are bought and sold. Exchanges provide the infrastructure, rules, and oversight necessary for fair and orderly trading.

    <ul className="text-slate-300 text-sm space-y-1">

      <li>Market makers and specialists</li>
      <li>Auction-based price setting</li>
      <li>Real-time settlement systems</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
